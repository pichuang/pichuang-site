[American Wire Gauge, AWG][1] 是區分導線的直徑標準，針對 DAC 常見的規範節錄如下：

| Cable OD| Wire Gauge |
|---------|------------|
|30 AWG   |0.0100 in   |
|28 AWG   |0.0126 in   |
|26 AWG   |0.0159 in   | 
|24 AWG   |0.0201 in   |
